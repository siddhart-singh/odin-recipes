Protein 373.2K Water

Description

This recipe is an art form the encapsulates everything wrong with the society. It is a reflection of how dinosaurs use to eat and drink a chilled beer after working fulltime in factories.

Ingredients

  • Not warm water
  • 1 Scoop of your favorite protein powder.
  • Metal utensil capable of withstanding some heat.
  • Heat source

Instructions

  1. Fill up the metal utensil with not warm water.
  2. Place the utensil on heat source and wait until bubbles arrise.
  3. Pour out the freshly prepared 372.2K water in to a cup or a glass and add 1 scoop of protein powder, enjoy!!
